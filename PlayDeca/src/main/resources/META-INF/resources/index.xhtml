🏰 Welcome to PlayDeca - Historic Minecraft Server

PlayDeca has a rich history in the Minecraft community. Originally starting as Zarkov in 2011, evolving into Octa.in (2012-2016), and now PlayDeca. We've been providing a stable, quality Minecraft experience for over a decade!

⛏️ Vanilla Survival Experience

Experience Minecraft as it was meant to be played. Our stable vanilla server combines the classic survival experience with carefully selected plugins to enhance gameplay without ruining the core Minecraft experience.

👥 Established Community

Join our growing community of Minecraft enthusiasts. From veterans who've played since the Zarkov and Octa.in days to new players discovering our server, we welcome all who appreciate a quality survival experience built on years of stability.
